Donuts

Project information

  • Category: Animation
  • Project date: December 2022

A short animation film expressing humanity through random acts of kindness. This project required the use of Adobe Illustrate, Adobe Photoshop, After Efects, Keynote, and iMovie. The score was borrowed from Danilo Stankovic, Pieces. The goal of this film is to inspire others to start their ripple of kindness.
